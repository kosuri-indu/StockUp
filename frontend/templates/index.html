Fridge Peek

Dive into the fun of keeping track of your fridge’s contents with Fridge Peek! This lively feature offers a dynamic view of your inventory, so you always know what’s on hand. Make fridge management enjoyable and keep your meals organized with ease.

Shelf-Alert

Be the ultimate food guardian with Shelf-Alert. This cheerful feature alerts you before items expire, so you can use up your food in time and keep waste at bay. Enjoy peace of mind with timely freshness reminders and a well-organized kitchen.

Safeguard your favorite recipes and culinary creations in one secure place. Dish Vault makes it easy to store and retrieve your top dishes whenever you need them.

Recipie Genie

Let your meals shine with Recipe Genie! This vibrant feature illuminates new recipe ideas based on what’s in your fridge, making every meal an opportunity for culinary delight. Spark your creativity and discover new flavors with a dash of brilliance.
